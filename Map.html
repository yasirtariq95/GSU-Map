About

The webpage is an interactive map for Georgia State University Students. The map is still in an interface phase and more updates will come in the future with aditional features. Currently, the user is able to move the character around the streets in the map, and retrive additonal information about some of the various buildings around Georgia State University. This webpage would be especially useful to new students who would like to know how to navigate around the university and get more information about the more important buildings on campus. As of right now, the map is a static display of the area West of 25 Park Place, South of the Honors College, East of I-75, and North of Grady Hospital. In future updates, there are plans to expand the map to include more locatons in the University.
